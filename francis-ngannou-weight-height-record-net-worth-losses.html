    Francis Zavier Ngannou is a Cameroonian mixed martial artist.

    He currently competes in the heavyweight division for the Ultimate Fighting Championship, where he is the current UFC Heavyweight Champion.

    He is known for being the hardest recorded puncher in the world.

    Francis Ngannou Weight

    Francis Ngannou weighs 257 lb (117 kg; 18 st 5 lb).

    Francis Ngannou Height

    Francis Ngannou stands at a height of 6 feet 4 inches (193 cm) tall.

    Francis Ngannou Record

    Francis Ngannou’s current professional record stands at seventeen wins, three losses, and zero draws (17-3-0).

    As of 2 November 2021, he is #4 in the UFC men’s pound-for-pound rankings.

    Francis Ngannou Net Worth

    Francis Ngannou has an estimated net worth of $5 million.

    Francis Ngannou Losses

    Francis Ngannou has lost thrice in his cage-fighting career.

    The first man to ever hand Ngannou a defeat was Zoumana Cisse at 100% Fight – Contenders 21 way back in 2013.

    Ngannou faced Stipe Miocic for the UFC Heavyweight Championship on 20 January 2018, at UFC 220 and lost the fight via unanimous decision.

    Also, Ngannou faced Derrick Lewis on 7 July 2018, at UFC 226 and lost the fight.
