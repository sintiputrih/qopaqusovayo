    Tiffany Lane Wiki Biography

    Tiffany Lane was born on 16 August 1974, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania USA, of African-American and German descent. Charli is a rapper and songwriter, best known for her Grammy nominated work. Her stage name comes from the film “The Long Kiss Goodnight”, specifically Geena Davis’ character. All of her endeavors have helped put her net worth to where it is today.

    How rich is Charli Baltimore? As of mid-2016, sources inform us of a net worth that is at $500,000, mostly earned through her career in music. She’s worked with big names in the industry and has made several collaborations, including releasing several albums and mixtapes featuring other artists. A lot of her singles have found popularity, and as she continues her career it is expected that her wealth will increase.

    Charli attended Peirce College, and after graduating went on to become a certified paralegal. In 1995, she met The Notorious BIG and had a romantic relationship with him which eventually introduced her to the music industry. One of her first appearances was in the music video “Get Money” alongside Junor MAFIA, and she then released her first video, “Money” which became a hit; the song was part of the soundtrack of the movie “Woo”. Eventually, Baltimore started working on her debut album entitled “Cold as Ice” which also featured artists NORE and Cam’ron. She would have several hits including “NBC”, “Stand Up” and “Pimp Da 1 U Love”.

    The album’s release encountered numerous problems, between artist and label, before it was promotionally released in 1999. However it didn’t make it to the public until 2009, finding availability as a digital download. She then took a five year break, later signing with Murder Inc. Records, but due to numerous problems she left the label. In 2006, she affiliated with The Black Wall Street Records and then collaborated with 50 Cent and The Game.

    Later on, Charli would make guest appearances in various songs such as “No One Does it Better”, “Down 4 U” and “Down Ass Bitch”. She also appeared in the track “Spending Time” and the remix of Ashanti’s “Rain on Me”. In 2003, thanks to her work, she received a nomination for a Grammy Award for her single “Diary”. Five years later, she signed with The Inc. Records and started working on the album “True Lies”. She then released an EP on iTunes which featured songs such as “Lose It””, “PS”, and “Come Test Us” which featured Lil Wayne. She then made a guest appearance in the Trey Songz video “If I Die Tonight”. Her net worth was rising slowly.

    In 2011, Charli worked with Sally Anthony and released “Machine Gun (Remix)”. She then worked on the mixtape entitled “Mallory Knox: Natural Born Khronicles” which was released in 2012, and then a new single entitled “All Lies”. In 2013, all references to her album “True Lies” were removed from social media and the album remains unreleased. She has also declined making radio appearances since 2012.

    For her personal life, it is known that Charli resides in Jersey City, New Jersey. She also has two daughters. she gave birth to her first child when she was only 14 years old, but for whatever reason, details of her relationships are kept strictly private.
