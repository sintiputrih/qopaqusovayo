    Anne Burrell And Stuart Claxton’s Relationship

    Stuart Claxton first met Anne Burrell on the dating app “Bumble” in 2018. Two years later, the couple got engaged on April 18, 2020, while quarantining with their family in Cazenovia.

    But although they held a small engagement ceremony, the duo always dreamed of having a grand wedding. So, they waited patiently for the COVID-19 restrictions to be eased. Finally, on October 16, 2021, the two tied the knot in a beautiful fall ceremony at Windridge Estates Redbarn 20 in Cazenovia, N.Y., the bride’s hometown.

    And the patience paid off! Their wedding was a moment to remember. Anne arrived down the aisle in a horse and carriage, accompanied at the altar by four bridesmaids including her sister, Jane, and close friend. 

    As for their attire, Anne wore a white dress made by Carolina Herrera, while Stuart rocked a black tuxedo by Ralph Lauren Purple Label and orange Nike high-tops. The guests were stunned, and also couldn’t be any happier with the food that was served. A roasted cauliflower steak for starter, Goat cheese and butternut squash ravioli for the past course, and a duo plate of braised short ribs for the entree.

    Following dinner, Stuart and Anne surprised the guest with their first dance to “On Top of the World” by Imagine Dragons. They later revealed that they attended dance classes and had a Broadway choreographer choreograph a dance for them.

    Some of the notable guests included tars Alex Guarnaschelli, Marc Murphy, Jeff Mauro, and Jenni ‘J Woww’ Farley.

    The couple followed the nuptials with a traditional honeymoon,  heading back to their recently renovated apartment in Brooklyn.

    Stuart Claxton Job: What Does He Do?

    Stuart Claxton has had quite an interesting job. He worked as an adjudicator for Guinness World Records for almost 13 years and was one of the founding members of their New York office since relocating from London in 2002.

    Also, Stuart was the marketing director for the company for North and South America, and has appeared on multiple popular shows like The Today Show, Good Morning America, The Early Show, Live! With Regis and Kelly, The Oprah Winfrey Show, and The Late Show with David Letterman.

    During his time with Guinness World Records, he has measured the world’s smallest and tallest men, tasted the most expensive sundae in the world, and witnessed the longest ramp-to-ramp jump on a motorcycle.

    Stuart retired from the company in 2015 and joined Time Inc as an executive director. He worked there for the next three years and jumped ship to Meredith Corporation. But within a year, Stuart left the company and started a career in ad sales marketing at Univison Communications Inc.

    In light of his education, Stuart has a bachelor’s degree in Spanish and Philosophy from Swansea University and a master’s degree in media management from the University of Stirling.

    Also, he was a certified IAB Digital Media Salesperson.

    By 2021, Stuart Claxton garnered a net worth of over $1 million.

    When Stuart wasn’t working, he liked to travel around or play golf.

    Trivia: According to “voices.com”, Stuart provided also voice-overs for projects.
