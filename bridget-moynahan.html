    Early Life and Education

    Bridget Moynahan was born on April 28, 1971, in New York City. Kathryn Bridget Moynahan is her given name. Edward Bradley Moynahan, her father, and Mary Bridget, her mother, are both Irish Americans. Her birthplace is in the United States, in Binghamton, New York. She is an American citizen. She is of White racial origin. Taurus is her zodiac sign. Andy and Sean are her two brothers. When she was about seven years old, her family relocated to Longmeadow, Massachusetts.

    She went to Massachusetts’ Longmeadow High School. She led the girls’ soccer, basketball, and lacrosse teams as captains. In 1989, she completed secondary school. She went on to the University of Massachusetts Amherst after that.

    Modeling Career:

    She began her modeling career after graduating from high school.

    She had gone to a modeling tryout with a friend in Springfield, Massachusetts. However, instead of her buddy, she was signed by the modeling agency.

    In Springfield, she began to feature in catalogs from department stores.

    She moved to New York when she was 18 years old.

    She subsequently began to feature in magazines such as Vogue, Elle, Glamour, and other well-known publications.

    She appeared on the covers of Vogue Paris in May 1993, Elle in October 1993, and Glamour in November 1993.

    Bridget Moynahan began acting in soap operas and television advertisements.

    She began taking classes in acting and art. She went to the Caymichael Patten Studio in New York to study acting.

    In November 2009, she secured a partnership with Garnier to promote their skincare products on television and in print (Ultra-Lift).

    Television Career:

    In 1999, she made her television debut as a guest star on the comedy series Sex and the City.

    Between 1999 and 2000, she had a recurrent role on Sex and the City.

    Between 2006 and 2007, she played Whitney Crane in Six Degrees as the main character.

    Six Degree was discontinued in May 2007 after one season.

    In 2010, she earned the lead part in the police drama Blue Bloods as Assistant District Attorney Erin Reagan.

    The ninth season of Blue Bloods is currently airing.

    Going to California and Eli Stone are two films in which she has appeared.

    She also appeared in the 2009 television film Bunker Hill.

    Film Career:

    In 2000, she made her feature film debut in Coyote Ugly.

    Serendipity, The Sum of All Fears, The Recruit, I, Robot, Lord of War, Grey Matters, Prey, Noise, Ramona and Beezus, John Wick: The Journey Home, and John Wick: Chapter 2 are some of her other notable films.

    She’s a happily married woman. Andrew Frankel, a businessman, is her husband. They married in the Hamptons on October 17, 2015.

    When she was cast as ADA Erin Reagan on Blue Bloods in 2010, she moved from Pacific Palisades, California to New York.

    In late 2010, she dated director McG.
