Whether our kids lose the remote for the TV, our partner misplaces their car keys or our best friend locks herself out (again), we’re always searching for ways to, well, stop searching. While there’s an entire encyclopedia of ways to teach ourselves to not misplace our wallets, phones and other bits and bots, the most effective way is to employ the use of a tool that’s designed to make losing stuff totally impossible. The best part? It’s a great way to never have to use Find My iPhone again.

    The Tile Starter Pack makes misplacing objects a thing of the past. Simply attach it to that one thing you’re always losing — be it a phone, a set of keys, or a kid’s favorite toy or book — and no need to ever worry about leaving it somewhere it shouldn’t be again. It’s even compatible with Amazon Alexa! All we have to do is ask Alexa to ask Tile to find the item we’re trying to source and it’ll let us know!

    How does it work? It’s super easy! We just pair the Tile with the Tile app on our smartphones and we’re ready to go. If the Tile is within the Bluetooth range, it will sound until the item is found. If your phone is the item you can’t hunt down, you can double-press the Tile button and it’ll make your phone ring, even if it’s turned on to silent mode. If you choose to have the Tile app running in the background, it’ll always remember the last place and time you had the thing you can’t find.
