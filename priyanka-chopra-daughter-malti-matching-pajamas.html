    Priyanka Chopra and her toddler daughter make for a stylish duo.

    The Citadel star recently shared a new selfie with two-year-old Malti Marie on Instagram, capturing a sweet mommy-and-me moment that involved matching pajama sets. In the picture, Chopra reclines on a couch and smiles at the camera while her daughter sits perched on her stomach, her gaze pointed off camera.

    While Chopra wore a collared button-up and Malti was in a long-sleeved top and leggings, both outfits were covered in a delicate white and light blue floral print. Both also had on gold jewelry, with Chopra wearing a simple chain bracelet and Malti sporting heart-shaped dangle earrings from Blomdahl and a stack of her own bracelets.

    More on the Chopra-Jonas Family

    “Her ❤️,” Chopra wrote in the caption. She added a note for her husband: “Miss u @nickjonas.”

    The singer responded by commenting under the photo, “My whole world.”

    Instagram

    Chopra and Jonas, who got married in 2018, welcomed Malti via a surrogate in January 2022. At the time, the two spoke candidly about that life-changing experience—as well as the subsequent health difficulties that left Malti undergoing treatment in the neonatal intensive care unit for more than 100 days.

    “She’s got me wrapped around her finger. I don’t even know how I’ll ever discipline her because I just don’t have it in me,” Chopra later reflected in an interview with Elle. “I was so close to losing her so many times that she can get away with anything and I just want to see her happy. I want her to be the happiest. She’s a super smiley, happy baby, and that’s all my goal is—to see her joyous. Every time she smiles, it lights up my world, and that’s all I want to do.”
