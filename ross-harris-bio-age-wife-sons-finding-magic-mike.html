    Ross Harris On Finding Magic Mike

    Meet Ross Harris one of the aspiring ‘Magic Mike’ in HBO Max’s new show.

    HBO Max has premiered a new show on 16 December 2021 titled ‘Finding Magic Mike’ mainly targeting the female demography among their overall viewers. The title, of course, speaks for itself and you might have at least a faint idea about the concept of the show, if not let us assist you further.

    In the show, you’ll get to witness some hot men, sexy dancing, and getting vulnerable, while discussing their feelings. The show runs for seven-episode and follows 10 non-professional dancers or strippers who are narrowed down from the group of 50. The winner of the show will bag a $100,000 cash prize and the opportunity to perform in the ‘Magic Mike Live’ stage show in Las Vegas.

    The episodes of the show reveal the contestants rehearsing increasingly challenging choreography, culminating in one big performance at the end of each episode. The show also involved actor Adam Rodriguez who appears on the show as a mentor. And the show is produced by none other than Channing Tatum, the real magic mike.

    Ross Harris Sons

    Ross Harris is a father of two beloved sons. Although he has featured both of his sons once on his social media he didn’t have many details on them. The sole reason for that was to protect them. His oldest son is around 6 years old whereas his youngest son Will is 20 months old. In the post from November 2021, he shared that Will was airlifted to Children’s Hospital in Little Rock late on November 11th with a severely low blood count.

    After being transferred there and receiving 3 blood transfusions, the doctors have told him that it was most likely leukemia. The first biopsy was inconclusive due to an insufficient sample. He shared that more tests would be conducted and another biopsy will likely be conducted on the 15th of November.

    Ross also shared that Will loves his elder brother a lot and also loves nature like Ross.

    Does Ross Harris Have A Wife?

    Ross Harris is likely not married. When he talked about his son Will, at the end of the post he referred to Will’s mother as “his mother” not his wife. We remain clueless on whether Ross had both of his sons from the same relationship or a different one. Other than the post from November about his son Will, he hasn’t mentioned his partner or baby mother anywhere.

    But, one thing that we know about Ross is he is a true gentleman.

    Ross has a TikTok (@marauder.fit) where he made one video about chivalry. He posted a clip from another TikTok content creator who asked “do you think women are killing chivalry?” He answered saying no and further added that it is not possible for women to kill chivalry.

    Ross further explained that chivalry is not contingent upon whether or not you get laid at the end of the day by your good actions. He shared that chivalry was 100% giving respect to women and everyone else regardless of how they treat you.

    And if a person is dictating whether or not you treat someone well based on their response to them then it has nothing to do with chivalry. Ross added further that the person who claimed to be chivalrous was just trying to play a nice guy to get laid and that person was never chivalrous from the very start.
