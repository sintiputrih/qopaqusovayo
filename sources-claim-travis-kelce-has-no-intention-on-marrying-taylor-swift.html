    Since Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift made their relationship official in September 2023, they have become one of the most famous couples in entertainment and sports. While Swift is arguably the more famous of the two, Kelce's 2024 Super Bowl win and his association with his pop star girlfriend have seen him experience a surge in popularity.

    Due to the couple's popularity, many fans are naturally anxious for the pair to take their relationship to the next level. While there have been countless rumors as of late that Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift are on the verge of getting engaged and later married, recently, several insider sources have spoken to the media to deny such claims.

    Travis Kelce Sources Deny Rumors He Plans On Asking Taylor Swift To Marry Him

    There is no denying that Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift have had their fair share of romantic relationships which have sadly ended in heartbreak. Prior to dating Kelce, Swift was in a brief romance with rock singer Matt Healy and, before him, she was in a six-year relationship with actor Joe Alwyn. Besides Swift, Kelce's most public relationships have previously been with sports reporter Kayla Nicole and social media influencer Maya Benberry.

    While Swift and Kelce have only been dating for a short time, the pair appear to be very much in love. Case in point, the couple were photographed embarking on a romantic rendezvous in Lake Como this past week. The two were even spotted sharing a kiss and going for a stroll hand in hand. As the couple prepares to travel to Italy next for Taylor Swift's Eras Tour, rumors have been swirling for months that they could become engaged very soon.

    A source close to the couple recently told Entertainment Tonight, "Taylor and Travis are doing amazing, and their loved ones see an engagement coming sooner than later. They make a great match and there's no question about that."

    However, new reports tell an entirely different story about the future of the superstar couple's relationship.

    According to an interview with Us Weekly, a source close to Travis Kelce disclosed to the publication that, "Travis has no plans on proposing to Taylor. Another source stated, "It's not even on his radar. Marriage is something he takes very seriously and not something he would ever just jump into without giving it some careful consideration." A separate source claimed that while Kelce 'cares very deeply' about the Bejeweled songstress 'he's just not there yet' when it comes to asking her to marry him.

    Travis Kelce And Taylor Swift Views On Marriage

    While countless rumors continue to swirl about whether Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift will become engaged, the pair have occasionally spoken about their individual views on marriage.

    Joe Alwyn is supposedly enjoying being single while rumors swirl that Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce might get engaged soon.

    In an 2012 interview with The Hollywood Reporter, Swift relayed that she did want to get married one day and added, “Being a mom full-time, doing everything for my kids, having a bunch of them. One day, I’m sure.“

    As for his own views on marriage, Travis Kelce has largely stayed quiet on the matter. However, a few months ago, he stated that he and Taylor Swift were on the same page when it comes to getting married and that they were not in a rush to walk down the aisle just yet. He added that they were currently enjoying their time together and taking things one step at a time and appreciated the support from the fans.
