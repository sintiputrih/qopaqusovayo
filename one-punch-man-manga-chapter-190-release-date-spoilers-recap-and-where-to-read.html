    One Punch Man is a popular manga created by ONE. It all started back in 2009 when ONE began posting the series on his personal blog. People loved it so much that in 2012, Shueisha’s Young Jump Web Comics officially published it. The story revolves around Saitama, a superhero who can defeat anyone with just one punch. But here’s the catch: he’s bored because he always wins effortlessly and can’t find opponents who challenge him. This manga became a sensation, and it even got adapted into an anime series. One Punch Man has taken the world by storm, with merchandise, spin-offs, and video games being created. The manga is still ongoing, and fans eagerly await the weekly release of new chapters.

    In this article, we’ll be taking a look at what to expect from One Punch man 190, including Raw Scan Release date, Reddit Spoilers, release date, and where to read.

    One Punch man Manga Chapter 190 Release Date

    Exciting news for One Punch Man fans! The eagerly awaited Chapter 190 is set to be released on August 09, 2023, right at midnight. The author has officially confirmed this update, so be sure to mark your calendars and prepare for the highly anticipated new chapter. The wait is almost over.

    The publication of the issue is scheduled for the following times in the relevant time zones:

    • Pacific Daylight Time: August 09, 2023, Wednesday, 3:00 AM

    • Eastern Daylight Time: August 09, 2023, Wednesday, 6:00 AM

    • Central European Time: August 09, 2023, Wednesday, 12:00 PM

    • Indian Standard Time: August 09, 2023, Wednesday, 3:30 PM

    • Japanese Standard Time: August 09, 2023, Wednesday, 7:00 PM

    • Korean Standard Time: August 09, 2023, Wednesday, 7:00 PM

    • Australia Central Daylight Time: August 10, 2023, Thursday, 7:30 PM

    • Chinese Standard Time: August 10, 2023, Thursday, 6:00 PM

    Where to read One Punch Man Latest Manga Chapter

    If you want to catch up on previous chapters of One Punch Man, you can read the raw format on Tonarinoyj website. However, for English readers, the Viz Media website and app are the best options. They provide the series in English, but please be aware that the latest chapter, Chapter 190, might not be available immediately.

    One Punch Man Chapter 190 Summary

    In the latest chapter of One Punch Man, we witness an intriguing encounter between Atomic Samurai and King. With Bang’s retirement from the Hero Association, Atomic Samurai seeks a new formidable rival, and King catches his attention. The disciples hear the “King Engine” start, a sign of his nervousness transforming into readiness for a fight. King’s power is often associated with Saitama’s recorded feats against monsters, especially his Ultimate Purgatory Hellfire Burst in Chapter 154, which was actually done by Garou.

    Atomic Samurai decides to challenge King, leading to a showdown outside. The confrontation escalates when Atomic Samurai throws an apple towards King, urging him to approach like a monster. However, King is overwhelmed by doubts and feels pressured to reveal his true self. He struggles with the situation, even asking for help, and Iaian notes the apparent openings in King’s defense, reminiscent of how Garou initially perceived him.

    King attempts to use Talk No Jutsu to escape, but his insincerity is evident. The chapter ends with an epic display of emotions and anticipation, leaving fans excited to see how this encounter will unfold.
