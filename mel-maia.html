    Career and Achievements

    Mel began her acting journey at the age of 7 in 2011 as Nina with a popular show “O Relógio da Aventura”. Her acting in the show was commendable and gave her popularity around Brazil as a child artist. In the following year, she appeared as Rita Fonseca de Souza in “Avenida Brasil”. The show was commercially successful. With rising fame, Mel acted in several popular shows including Joia Rara (2013), Além do Tempo (2015), Liberdade, Liberdade (2016), Deus Salve o Rei (2018), A Dona do Pedaço (2019), and many.

    In 2012 Mel received her first-ever Prêmio Extra de Televisão Award for her fabulous acting in Avenida Brasil. In the following year, she won the Prêmio Contigo! in Best Child Actress for the same show. In 2013 again, Mel won the Melhores do Ano Awards in the Best Child Actress category. 

    In addition to this, with rising popularity as an actress, Mel has garnered millions of followers on various social media platforms including Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and Twitter. On Instagram, she has amassed more than 10m followers and 5m followers on TikTok. Subsequently, various apparel, makeup, and swimwear brands have appointed Mel as the face of their products.
