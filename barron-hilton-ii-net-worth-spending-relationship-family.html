    The Hilton's are known to be one of the wealthiest families in America. Almost every household is familiar with their name.

    Paris Hilton came into the limelight through her reality shows and party lifestyle in the early 2000s. Even though Paris is the most famous, she's not an only child.

    According to Oldest.com, Paris Hilton has 3 siblings making them four in totality. Paris is the firstborn. She was born in 1981, followed by Nicky Hilton Rothschild who was born in 1983. Nicky is married to James Rothschild, and he hails from a wealthy family as well. Barron Hilton II was born in 1989 making him the 3rd born. And then their youngest brother, Conrad Hughes Hilton was born in 1994.

    Even though Paris Hilton says now that becoming a billionaire doesn't mean as much to her as it used to some time back, people aren't buying it. Who wouldn't want to be wealthy?

    Barron Hilton II isn’t as eminent as his older sister Paris is, but he is still living an extremely lavish life.

    With that in mind, it is expected for Barron Hilton II to live a certain level of lifestyle that only less than 5% of people globally get to enjoy. The rest only get to dream about.

    8 Who Is Barron Hilton II?

    Barron Hilton II was born on November 7th, 1989 to Kathy Hilton and Richard Hilton. His childhood is not out there for the public to peruse. Although as a young adult, he loved to party a lot.

    According to Daily Mail, In 2013, Barron got into a fight in a club while he was out parting. He claimed to have thought that the mastermind of the altercation was Lindsay Lohan.

There was a time his Instagram bio introduced him as the Senior Vice President of the Beverly Hills luxury real estate company Hilton & Hyland. The position may not have changed, but his Instagram bio did. Additionally, Barron Hilton II is an actor and a director. He was featured in the 2019 film, Spring to Winter.

    7 Who Is Barron Hilton II Married To?

    Socialite Barron Hilton II is married to Tessa Hilton who was previously known as Tessa Gräfin von Walderdorff. They met in the Caribbean where they crossed paths in a restaurant on The Island of St. Barts in 2016. Barron and Tessa were staying there for New Year’s Eve.

    Moving forward, Barron engaged Tessa in September 2017 when Tessa took to Instagram to share the exciting news with a lovely picture of them kissing and a caption saying, “Better than a fairytale... engaged to my best friend, my soul mate, the love of my life. Forever and for always.”

    Together, Barron Hilton II and Tessa Hilton have two children. According to People, their first child, daughter Milou came into the world in March 2020. Two years later in Sept 2022, the couple welcomed their son, Caspian.

    6 What Is Barron Hilton’s Net worth?

    According to Celebrity Net Worth, Barron Hilton II has a net worth of $5 million. We know you probably expected much more because of his grandfathers’ wealth, but this amount is his hard-earned money. As a real estate mogul, Barron has done so much for himself.

    His wife, Tessa Hilton isn’t lagging. She also has the same net worth of $5 million. Tessa produces her music and is also a DJ. Judging from her Instagram bio, Tessa says she is a representative for Estate Five Management.

    5 Barron Hilton II Is A Foodie

    This may come as a shock to many, but just like the rest of us, Barron Hilton II is a foodie. His love for different flavors busting in his mouth caused him to once open an Instagram page. According to Style, the Instagram page that goes by the name barronbites once belonged to Barron Hilton II. The Instagram page is still up, but nobody is running it. In fact, it has been quite a while since its last post. With the tagline, my culinary quest one bite at a time, the pictures portrayed on the page indicate that his food is of top-notch and the highest quality.

    Additionally, Barron goes to eat at expensive restaurants, one of them being Dokebi Bar And Grill.
