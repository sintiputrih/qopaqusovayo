What is Knicks short for?

11 Feb 2024, 00:00

The term "Knicks" is a shortened form of "Knickerbockers," which is a word used to describe the early Dutch settlers in New York. The Knickerbockers were a prominent social class in the 19th century, known for their distinctive style of dress, which included wearing knee-length breeches, often referred to as "knickerbockers." This term eventually became associated with New York City and, in particular, its basketball team, the New York Knicks.
