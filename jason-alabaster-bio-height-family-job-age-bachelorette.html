    Jason Alabaster Family

    Jason Alabaster was born to his parents Mike and Karen Alabaster.

    As of July 2022, Mike is 70 years old while Karen turned 64. Mike is a urologist by profession. He founded Southeast Urology Network in July 1982. He also founded other institutions such as American Surgical Centers and MD Services Group.

    Mike completed his study of urology at the University of Tennessee Medical Center at Memphis. He graduated manga cum laude from Memphis State University. He performed his internship and residency in surgery at Baptist Memorial Hospital in Memphis before completing a residency in urology at the University of Tennessee, where he was Chief Resident from 1980-82.

    Mike is also a Clinical Professor at the University of Tennessee Department of Urology and is board certified by the American Board of Urology. He is known for bringing the first kidney stone lithotriptor to Memphis, starting the cryosurgery program at Baptist Hospital, and setting up one of the few U.S. HIFU (High-Frequency Ultrasound) prostate cancer clinical trials in Memphis.
