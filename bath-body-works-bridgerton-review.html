    My dearest reader, I am pleased to share the splendid news I have for you today that will surely delight the ton. Bath & Body Works recently released a Bridgerton collection in collaboration with Netflix, that smells absolutely divine. Now, I’m sure most of you Bridgerton fans may have already known this, but for those that don’t, I’m here to give you an inside scoop on what this new collab is all about. As someone who’s tried the latest assortment, I can attest that these unique, sweet-smelling aromas are some of the best we’ve ever gotten from the fragrance brand. 

    Related story Amazon Has an Unbeatable Prime Exclusive Deal on a Set of UGG Cooling Bed Sheets for Just $19

    Going off the scent’s names, one can assume they’re inspired by some of the series’ most prominent characters and places. However, as someone who’s watched (and re-watched) both seasons, I imagined the scents to represent those utterly different from their namesake titles, which I’m sure will surprise the ton.

    Below, find my thoughts on some of the new products from the collection I tried, which scents I find the absolute best, and who they really represent (in this writer’s opinion).

    Diamond of the Season – Daphne Bridgerton

    Diamond of Season 3-Wick Candle

    As the first Diamond of the Season, I couldn’t help but think of Daphne Bridgerton upon smelling this candle. It has really bright notes of sparkling peach, spring daffodil, and radiant jasmine. The sparkling peach really comes through at first whiff and reminds me of a sweet peach bellini on a hot summer day. It also brings back memories of all the bejeweled adorned dresses and stunning diamonds Daphne wore in the first season. The light floral scents remind me of her sweet yet noticeable demeanor that made her standout in the first place as the Diamond of the Season. This fragrance is truly quite remarkable.

    Bridgerton Study – Simon Bassett, Duke of Hastings

    Bridgerston Study 3-Wick Candle

    And what is a diamond without her gorgeous husband? Given the name, Bridgerton Study, I would’ve assumed this fragrance would remind me of Antony Bridgerton, but instead it makes me think of the young Duke of Hastings. It has rugged scents of smooth amber and oakwood that’s paired with dried orchids, offering a warm and sensual aroma. I couldn’t help but think of Simon. With all the … um, hot and bothered moments of the show, there were plenty that came to mind when taking this fragrance in. One in particular (that’s relatively appropriate to share) is when the Duke plays with the spoon early on in the first season, IYKYK. I burned this one at night and it truly felt like a warm embrace while I got ready for bed, everything I could imagine Daphne experiences when close to Simon. Definitely one of my favorites!

    Danbury Shortbread – Edwina Sharma

    Danbury Shortbread Gentle & Clean Foaming Hand Soap

    $4 $9 56% off

    While I can totally see why this is inspired by Lady Danbury’s rather sweet side, the incredibly sweet and intoxicating aroma reminded me so much of Edwina Sharma. Her soft and timid personality made me think she was the sweetest girl on the whole show. It has comforting notes of whipped vanilla, almonds, and sugar crystals that remind me of a delicious fresh-baked cookie with every whiff. This fragrance comes in a Wallflower refill, hand sanitizer, candle, and of course, hand soap. I received the candle and hand soap, and while both smell wonderful, I prefer the fragrance in the hand soap. As sweet as it is, it can be a little overwhelming in a candle. I think the hand soap leaves a very nice and comforting scent behind instead.

    Wisteria Garden – Eloise Bridgerton

    Wisteria Garden Gentle & Clean Foaming Hand Soap

    $4 $9 56% off

    While her quest to not participate in society and not fate the doom of being married might be a bit annoying to some (i.e., Penelope), I found Eloise to be a nice breath of fresh air, just like this fragrance. Wisteria Garden has refreshing notes of sweet rosewood, English rain, and lemon petals that instantly made me think of a sunny spring day. The lightness and how bright the fragrance is made me feel more productive and want to focus while working from home, similar to Eloise, who’s always reading, writing, and being more curious by the moment.

    Queen Charlotte’s Tea – Kate Sharma

    Queen Charlotte’s Tea3-Wick Candle

    I’m sure this fragrance might remind those of Queen Charlotte, but the aromatic notes of black tea leaves, bold citrus, and rich bergamot reminded me of nothing but Kate Sharma. We see Kate making and enjoying numerous cups of delicious tea all throughout season two. She even teaches viewers how to make a proper cup of Indian tea. Similar to the Wisteria Garden fragrance, this scent is also a welcoming breath of fresh air that feels absolutely delightful. This one is hands down my favorite scent, and I can’t wait to stock up on more candles before they sell out.
