    Samantha Harris is a well-known American actress, model, entertainment reporter, and television presenter who has participated in a variety of motion picture and television productions.

    She achieved her greatest fame as Tom Bergeron’s co-host on Dancing with the Stars from seasons two through nine.

    In addition to her work as a host, Harris has acted in a number of movies and TV episodes, such as Reefer Madness, Beautiful, Drop Dead Gorgeous, Surviving Gilligan’s Island, D3: The Mighty Ducks, and CSI: Crime Scene Investigation.

    A accomplished model, Harris has posed on the covers of publications like Muscle & Fitness: HERS, FHM, SELF, and SHAPE.

    She has also served as the brand ambassador for Palmer’s Cocoa Butter Formula, and in 2007 and 2008, she received back-to-back Crown Awards for “Best Hairstyle on Television.”

    Harris married Michael Hess in 2005, and the two of them had two daughters together. Harris gave birth to their first daughter in 2007, and as a result, she was unable to watch the debut of Dancing with the Stars season five.

    After Drew Lachey stepped in for the first three weeks, she returned to the program in October 2007. She gave birth to their second daughter three years later.
