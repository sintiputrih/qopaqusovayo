    White sneakers are the universal closet staple that transcend personal style and trending aesthetics. Through all of my phases — preppy, maximalist, casual-cool — there has always been a pair of the classic shoes in rotation because no matter what my closet looked like that year, they went with it. 

    Adidas’ Stan Smiths sneakers have been an ‘It’ shoe since they were released in the ‘60s. The style is somehow both timeless and always on-trend. They look great with a pair of jeans and a white T-shirt, but can also make elevated pieces feel a little cooler. Meghan Markle has been known to keep it casual with the shoes, wearing them with joggers and jeans, while Amal Clooney proved they can make dressed-up pieces feel more everyday-appropriate when she paired hers with a mid-length, sleeveless sweater dress. 

    And right now, the iconic style that more than 6,600 Amazon shoppers reach for is on rare discount at Amazon.

    Adidas Stan Smith Sneakers

    As someone who’s worn Stan Smiths for years, I can speak to the comfort of the sneakers. I could wear this shoe all day without any aches or pain, even when working retail jobs where I was standing for up to nine hours. One shopper noted that even after repeatedly wearing them during 10-hour shifts, the sneakers “kept their support,” while another person said they can “walk for hours” in these sneakers thanks to their comfort and “perfect fit.” 

    And like everyone else who reaches for their sneakers, I adore their versatility. This is a shoe I can (and have) worn with sweatpants as well as dresses. I love the look of these with a pair of loose jeans and a blazer — the casual elements balance the sophistication for a look that feels unbelievably cool. Other customers say that they wear the style with wide-leg trousers and skirts, with one person saying that they’re so versatile, they wear them to both the office and weekend brunches.
