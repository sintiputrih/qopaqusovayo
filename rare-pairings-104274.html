    These are the weirdest pokémon ships I support, they're so rare that I'm sure they don't even have a ship name:

    - Lt. Surge x Erika: I know that they never interacted with each other on screen but they are my favorite gym leaders and I like to pair opposite personalities, they could have an interesting dynamic.

    - Bruno x Karen: For basically the same reasons as above, I find these two pairings very cute even if I know they're impossible.

    These are not that rare, but I don't think they have many supporters:

    - Faba x Wicke: They have little moments that make me ship them.

    - Lana x Sophocles: They also have little moments that make me ship them, I find both pairings really cute.

    - Lance x Clair: They ruined it by making them cousins, I'll just pretend they aren't cousins .

    These are ships between pokémon that although they're not rare, people don't usually think much about them:

    - Ash's Bulbasaur x Florinda's Gloom: They were adorable in that episode .

    - Magearna x Volcanion: You know something was there .

    - Wigglytuff x Chatot: From the Mistery Dungeon games, this is canon and you can't tell me othewise .

    - Mewtwo x Genesect: That fall to the earth in the movie was very suspicious .

    - Clemont's Bunnelby x Serena's Sylveon: It was cute.

    - Loudred x Sunflora: Another cute ship from the Mystery Dungeon Games.
