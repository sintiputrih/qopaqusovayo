    Meet Adair Curtis, The Interior Designer On Netflix’s Instant Dream Home

    Adair Curtis (no relation to Erik Curtis) was finally very excited when the world was finally able to see him and his team go to some of the absolutely outrageous lengths to completely transform unsuspecting people’s homes in just 12 hours of time.

    With the expertise of having designed the homes of stars like Taraji P. Henson, Gabrielle Union, Sanaa Lathan, and many more, Adair brings himself on Instant Dram Home; in total 8 episodes.

    In each episode of this show, Adair and his team of renovators secretly transforms a worthy family’s home from “drab to fab” within only 12 hours.

    So, in Netflix’s words, it is a part home renovation show and part heist movie.

    How Much Is Adair Curtis Net Worth?

    Adair Curtis reportedly had more than $800K as net worth as of 2022.

    Known for his clean aesthetic and elegant approach to styling and living, Adair is a celebrity interior designer. He and his husband, Jason Bolden, are the creative directors and co-founders of JSN STUDIO (since June 2014), a multidisciplinary design and styling firm based in Los Angeles.

    Adair’s keen eye and refined skills have not only made him one of the most sought-after talents in the interior design and entertainment industries but placed him on the coveted “1stDibs 50 2022 Honoree” list.

    Adair says it is the showrooms of the Pacific Design Center that gets his creative juices flowing. And, it is West Hollywood’s small design businesses and community of entrepreneurs that inspire him most.

    As per LinkedIn, Adair studied Digital Media Marketing Strategy at New York University only for a year in 2012.

    Adair Curtis Family

    Adair’s mother is no more. Yet, we get to see memories of her on Adair’s social media every now and then.

    Adair Curtis Partner

    Adair Curtis has been married to Jason Bolden for almost a year now. On 17 August 2021, they celebrated their 9th wedding anniversary when Adair said that he would not want to be on this journey with anyone else.

    Like Adair, his partner, Jason is an American entrepreneur, creative director, fashion stylist, and interior designer widely regarded for his clean aesthetic and elegant approach to style and living.

    Jason, along with Adair, is consistently called upon to reconceive images and create new looks for Hollywood’s superstars. Together, this pair also starred in the hit Netflix series, Styling Holywood. They were introduced in the show’s pilot season in August 2019 as the interior designer portion.

    Jason, on IG @jasonbolden, turned 40 on 27 February 2022.

    Jason and Adair are also joined by their son Mr. Arrow Fox in life. The young one was born on 24 May 2021.

    At this time, Adair had designed an updated family room in their home in a new partnership with the Samsung Frame TV. “I love designing for our clients but I especially love designing for my most demanding clients — my family,” he had gushed about the project to PEOPLE.

    So, since Arrow’s arrival Adair and his partner have been basking in the joy of fatherhood.

    Adair not long ago shared that he is obsessed with his child. To the extent that he is like he cannot have this little one in the world without a brother or sister. He and his partner are already thinking that their son is in need of a sibling. They especially thought this because Adair grew up one of five and Jason, one of five siblings.
