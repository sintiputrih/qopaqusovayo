    Who Are Margo And Ally From Bake Off: The Professionals?

    Get to know Margo and Ally who are competing on Bake Off: The Professionals. The first episode of a new season of British Bake Off premiered on July 2023 and we are here to fill the gaps in their personal and professional life.

    Here we discuss their age, marriage, last name, and more.

    Margo And Ally On Bake Off: The Professionals

    The new season of Bake Off is upon us dubbed “The Professionals” in 2023. The new season premiered on 4 July and fans have been graced with a sweet treat.

    The contestants are paired in teams and in this piece, we will discuss Margo and Ally. The chef, affectionately referred to by her coworkers as “Sergeant” Margo, relocated to London from Poland in 2014. She completed her training at Le Cordon Bleu before starting her first shift on a busy Mother’s Day at London’s Balthazar restaurant.

    Margo has been employed by the award-winning Lexington Catering for a year, where she collaborates closely with her coworker Ally to manage the pastry department. They boldly called their pastry area Queer Corner and decorated it with everything rainbow during Pride Month.

    Both members of the LGBTQ+ community have proudly renamed their pastry sections, Queer Corner, adorning it with all things rainbow during pride month. When not busy in the kitchen, teammate Ally will be found guarding the goal of their five-a-side football team and recently, at an international tournament in Amsterdam, Ally came away with player of the tournament.

    Other contestants joining her this season are Andrew and Raf from Harwoods Patisserie, Annie and Subin from The Dorchester, Caroline, and Martin from Bisous Bisous, Mustapha and Tracy, Chantelle, and Kasia from Glenapp Castle, Martyn and Jenny, Mauro and Daniel from The Landmark London, Mayank and Dharma from St James’ Court, A Taj Hotel, Naira and Andrea, and Nicoletta and Georgina from Meraki Baking Studio.

    Benoit Blin and Cherish Finden will judge once more, while Liam Charles will join them as hosts once more. The star of Ted Lasso and the most recent season of Strictly Come Dancing, Ellie Taylor, will be his co-presenter this time around.

    Per Independent.ie, Margo and Ally were shooting shot for first place, but they ended up becoming a runners-up in the first episode. Raf and Andrew prevailed. Martyn and Jenny were also told to go home. The other contestants weren’t quite as intriguing, and they kept viewers entertained throughout the first episode.

    Margo And Ally Career

    Learn about Margo and Ally’s career here,

    • Margo’s Career

    Margo started as a Pastry Chef at Maestra Restaurant in 2012 in Bydgoszcz, Kuyavian-Pomeranian District, Poland. After working for a little over two and a half years, she joined Balthazar as a pastry chef in March 2015. She worked there for a year.

    After that in June 2016, Margo joined Pastry Chef at Create Food till January 2018. She also worked at Wilson Vale Catering Management first as Chef De Partie and then as Sous Chef.

    In 2023, Margo is the founder of Magpies Patisserie (@themagpiespatisserie) in May 2020. She is also a head pastry chef at Lexington Catering from March 2022.

    Margo attended Nicolaus Copernicus University and Le Cordon Bleu London.

    • Ally’s Career

    Ally has shared posts about food here and there but there are no definitive career details available about her. On her IG, Ally shared that she is an assistant coach at Olympia Infinity Football Club (@olympiafc2022).

    On her IG bio, Ally stated that she is playing as a Goal Keeper at Collective Joy FC (@collectivejoyfc), East London women’s & non-binary grassroots football club. Proudly sponsored by @childresourcegroup.

    In September 2021, Ally posted on IG, “Only went and won a bloody award!! GK of the Season!!! Big up @addaikenny for his fabulous hard effort in training me week in week out! Big love to my  @super5league  and @claptoncfc  family!! #gkunion  #football  #goalkeeper #hardworkpaysoff #dreamteam #claptonqueers.”

    Margo And Ally Age

    • Margo

    Born before 1987, Margo is at least 36 years old.

    • Ally

    Born in February 1993, Ally is 30 years old. She celebrates her birthday on 23 February.

    Margo And Ally Last Name

    The last name of Margo and Ally are Kujawa and Walker, respectively. So Margo’s full name is Małgorzata Kujawa and Ally is All Walker.

    Margo And Ally Married

    • Margo

    Currently, there is no information about Margo’s relationship status. She is available on IG but she is private.

    • Ally

    Ally appears to be in a relationship. She has frequently appeared with her partner.

    Ally refers to her partner as “the babe” on most of her posts and hasn’t shared her name publicly. First appeared on an IG post in 2015, they have been together for more than 8 years.
