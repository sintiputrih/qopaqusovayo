    The net worth of Jorja Fox is estimated to be $6 million as of mid-2016. Born in New York City in 1968, Jorja Fox is known for her acting roles in popular TV series such as “ER,” “CSI: Crime Scene Investigation,” and “The West Wing,” which have all contributed significantly to her wealth.

    Apart from being an accomplished actress, Fox is also a producer, musician, and songwriter. Raised in Melbourne Beach, Florida, she struggled with overweight during her childhood but later pursued a career in modeling after studying drama at the Lee Strasberg Institute in New York.

    Her acting career took off in 1989 with a role in the film “The Kill-Off.” She subsequently appeared in several TV shows and movies, with notable roles in “Missing Persons,” “ER,” and Christopher Nolan’s “Memento.”

    Fox is widely recognized for her role as forensic scientist Sara Sidle in “CSI: Crime Scene Investigation,” which she portrayed from 2000 to 2015. Her dedication to animal advocacy is evident in her support for organizations like PETA and Animal Acres.

    In addition to her acting career, Fox has also ventured into production, co-executive producing the documentary “Roll with Me” in 2017 and producing “Lion Ark” and “How I Became an Elephant” in previous years.

    Despite her successful career, Jorja Fox maintains a relatively private personal life, with a strong passion for animal welfare and vegetarianism.

    Jorja Fox’s noteworthy contributions to the entertainment industry have earned her accolades, including the Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by an Ensemble in a Drama Series in 2005 for her role in “CSI: Crime Scene Investigation.”
