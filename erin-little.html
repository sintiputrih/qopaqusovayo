    Introduction :

    Erin Little is an award-winning American meteorologist and journalist for KMBC- TV.

    Education, Career, Salary and Net Worth :

    • Erin is originally from Lee’s Summit, Missouri. She attended the University of Missouri-Columbia in 1997 and graduated with a B.S in Atmospheric Sciences and Meteorology.
    • Her first meteorologist stint was with KOMU-TV as a weekend evening meteorologist right after graduation.
    • She then moved to WOI-TV in Des Moines, Iowa, as a weekday morning Meteorologist & Reporter.
    • Erin later headed to KMBC-TV in Kansas City, Missouri, followed by a year at KDVR – Fox31 before returning to KMBC.
    • After a good ten years at KMBC, Erin left in January 2017. She became a director at the Shawnee Mission School Board, heading district communications operations. This job did not dim her love for broadcasting, and Erin was back on air as a reporter and meteorologist for the KCTV5 News team in March 2018. Currently, she is employed as the Chief Meteorologist there.
    • On her return to television, Erin said she was “so excited to be back delivering Kansas City’s weather. She loves that she is part of people’s morning routine and helping them prepare for the day with her weather reports.
    • Erin won an Emmy Award in 2006 while with KMBC.

    Volunteer Work :

    • Erin has volunteered with Harvesters – The Community Food Network on their Check Out Hunger Campaign since 2008. The campaign raises awareness to prompt and donations to Harvesters.
    • She also volunteers for The Salvation Army “Adopt – A – Family” Campaign that supports families in need.

    Interesting Facts and Trivia :

    • Erin lives with her family, which includes her three daughters. Her close connection with her kids is evident in her social media posts. She counts Saturday snuggles in bed with her kids as her way to relax, and a children’s book ‘Goodnight Moon’ is a firm favorite.
    • In an interview, Erin gave a behind-the-screen glimpse of her life as a TV meteorologist. She wakes up at 1.00 am to get to the studio by 2.00 am. Her life at home with the kids starts at 10 am, where she juggles the demands of her children’s school and after-school activities before going to bed at 8 pm.
    • While she is not crazy about the hours, Erin treated her weather forecasting job as a public service – a way she could serve the community and help them plan their day. She further added she couldn’t have done all the juggling of her home and work life without support from her immediate family and her ‘work-family,’ who understand the pressures of insane hours.
    • Her favorite place to shop is the Downtown Lee’s Summit while her favorite place to eat is 3rd Street Social. She loves taking her kids out to the zoo and parks but thinks the kids playing on her driveway is magical as well.
    • Erin is a sports fan and is loyal to all Kansas City favorites such as the Royals, Chiefs, and Sporting KC.
    • Her favorite TV show is Scandal and her go-to song is My Way by Calvin Harris.
