    Melissa Etheridge Biography

    Melissa Etheridge is best known as a Rock Singer. Grammy Award-winning singer-guitarist and songwriter who is also a powerful advocate for women ‘s causes. She also composed music for the Weeds show. She was born on May 29, 1961 in Leavenworth, KS.     Melissa Etheridge Relationships Status, Girlfriends,

    The sexual identity of Melissa Etheridge is a lesbian. She came out as a lesbian in January 1993 to celebrate President Bill Clinton’s first inauguration, the Triangle Ball. She was a gay rights activist. She had a long-standing relationship with filmmaker Julie Cypher. Cypher gave birth to two children, Bailey Jean and Beckett. She became pregnant through artificial insemination with sperm donated by musician David Crosby. The couple was separated in 2000.
