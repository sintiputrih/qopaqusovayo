    Those who have the Halo: The Master Chief Collection have the next game out on PC, the first one to start it all, Halo: Combat Evolved. It’s the anniversary game, and it comes with plenty of updated graphics for those to relive the classic title. While the game’s multiplayer will be fun for players to go through with updated mechanics, the campaign is another adventure. How many missions are there available in the game?

    There are two campaign missions in total you can play through, either with a friend or by yourself.

    • Pillar of Autumn
    • Arriving on Halo
    • Truth and Reconciliation
    • The Silent Cartographer
    • Assault on the Control Room
    • 343 Guilty Spark
    • The Library
    • Two Betrayals
    • Keyes
    • The Maw

    You change up how these campaign missions feel by increasing the difficulty, using difficult skulls to change up the gameplay, and play with a friend. For those who are playing Halo: Combat Evolved on their PC, you will only be able to play the game co-op while online. There is no way to play local co-op or use any split screen functionality.

    You can purchase Halo: Combat Evolved as a standalone title on Steam or the Microsoft store, or you can choose to buy the entire Halo: The Master Chief Collection to have access to all of the Halo games, when they release. First, Halo: Reach comes out, followed by Halo: Combat Evolved, Halo 2, Halo 3, Halo 4, and then Halo 5.
