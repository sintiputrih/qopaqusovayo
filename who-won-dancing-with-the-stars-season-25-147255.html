    29 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Congratulations, Jordan Fisher and Lindsay Arnold! The 23-year-old actor and his pro partner, Lindsay, also 23, took home the coveted Mirror Ball Trophy on the Tuesday, Nov. 21 Dancing With the Stars Season 25 finale — and we couldn’t be happier for them! Jordan and Lindsay made it to the DWTS finale with other couples Frankie Muniz and Witney Carson and Lindsey Stirling and Mark Ballas. Violinist Lindsey and her partner, Mark, came in second place while actor Frankie and dancer Witney finished in third.

    After their big win, both Lindsay and Jordan took to social media to gush about their victory. “There aren’t words. There aren’t words to describe the feeling of going through BATTLE with my SISTER. Putting in all the time and effort and energy for 12 weeks, then to be rewarded for it after having the most incredible time?! Unbelievable. This is for you all. Thank you thank you thank you,” Jordan captioned a video of the two thanking their fans. Lindsay similarly wrote, “OH MY GOSH!!!!! WOW!!! I literally have no words!! These faces say it all. I am so unbelievably grateful for this season, this partner/brother of mine, the show @dancingabc, and all of you amazing fans who voted!! We did it!! 4, 3, 2, 1!!!!!!! Thank you thank you from the bottom of my heart I love you all and appreciate your support!!!”

    Season 25 of Dancing With the Stars began in mid-September with a total of 13 competing couples. Shark Tank star Barbara Corcoran and her pro partner Keo Motsepe were voted off first with singer-songwriter Debbie Gibson and dancer Alan Bersten eliminated second. During the third week of competition, former NBA star Derek Fisher and his partner Sharna Burgess got the boot while Pretty Little Liars actress Sasha Pieterse and Gleb Savchenko made it to Week 4.

    Next to go was singer Nick Lachey with his partner Peta Murgatroyd followed by a Week 6 double elimination of actress Vanessa Lachey and Maksim Chmerkovskiy and WWE superstar Nikki Bella and Artem Chigvintsev. Former NFL wide receiver Terrell Owens and dancer Cheryl Burke were voted off next in the seventh week and Paralympic swimmer Victoria Arlen and Val Chmerkovskiy left the competition during the semi-finals. The last pair to be eliminated was Drew and Emma on Part 1 of the finale episode, which aired on Monday, Nov. 20. Congratulations again to the Season 25 winners, Jordan and Lindsay!
