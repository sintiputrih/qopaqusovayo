    Quavious Keyate Marshall who goes by the stage name Quavo is an American rapper, co-founder and the leading man of the music group, Migos. Quavo was born on April 2, 1991, in Athens, Georgia in the United States.

    Quavo is also known to be the uncle of Takeoff, who recently passed away in Houston while with him and also a cousin of Offset. The three gentlemen made the group Migos.

    Away from the group called Migos, Quavo has solo songs such as open it up, stir fry, dead, walk it take it, bad and boujee, intentions and many others.
    Quavo is also an executive of the FCF Glacier boys, a 7-on-7 indoor football league which was created in 2017.

    The award-winning rapper Quavo was born to Edna Marshall. There is no information about his father.
