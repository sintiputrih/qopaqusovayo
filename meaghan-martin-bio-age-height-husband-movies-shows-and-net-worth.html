    Meaghan Martin Biography

    Meaghan Martin born Meaghan Jette Martin is an American actress and singer best known for her role as Tess Tyler in the Disney Channel television film, Camp Rock and its sequel. She starred in the ABC Family television series, 10 Things I Hate About You as Bianca Stratford.

    Meaghan Martin Early Career

    Martin began modeling when she was five years old. She also appeared in several community theater productions in Las Vegas such as Peter Pan and performed for her Showteam and later made her professional theater debut in a Los Angeles production of 13. Martin appeared in several television commercials like Cabbage Patch Kids and Barbie.

    Meaghan Martin Acting

    Martin began her acting career in 2007, such as the television programs Nickelodeon’s Just Jordan and CBS’s Close to Home, and also as a guest stint in the Disney Channel series The Suite Life of Zack & Cody. She eventually cast as the lead antagonist Tess Tyler in the Disney Channel original movie, Camp Rock, as Demi Lovato and the Jonas Brothers. In 2008, Martin was selected by TV Guide, as one of the “13 Hottest Young Stars to Watch”. She was the online host for the 2008 Disney Channel Games.

    Martin replaced Brittany Snow in all subsequent releases in Kingdom Hearts series where she voiced the character Naminé. Brittany Snow did not return to the series. Martin starred as Bianca (originated by Larisa Oleynik in the film version) in the ABC Family sitcom 10 Things I Hate About You. Martin also had the role of Megan Kennedy in the independent film Dear Lemon Lima. She returned to reprise her role as Tess Tyler in Camp Rock 2: The Final Jam, premiered on September 3, 2010 on Disney Channel. Martin played the main character Jo in the 2011 television film Mean Girls 2, the sequel of the 2004 film Mean Girls. Martin also played the role of Aubrey in the film Sironia.

    Martin appeared on June 16, 2011 for one night in Super Summer Theatre’s live production of “Annie” at Spring Mountain Ranch in Las Vegas, Nevada in the role of Star-To-Be. Martin is also starring in the web series Wendy as the title character together with Tyler Blackburn. Wendy is a dark twist on ‘Peter Pan’. The series premiered on September 15, 2011 on macys.com.

    Martin appeared in the 2013 film adaptation Geography Club as Trish, while she appeared in the thriller The Good Mother was aired on Lifetime Channel and cast as Julie #2 in MTV’s Awkward, replacing Sophie Tilson. In the PlayStation 4 exclusive Until Dawn, she voiced the character of Jessica. Martin trained as an actor in London, England and in September 2016 completed her Masters in Classical Acting at the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art (LAMDA).

    Meaghan Martin Music

    Martin recorded two songs in Camp Rock, “2 Stars” and “Too Cool”. She sang a version of “When You Wish Upon a Star” for the 2009 re-release of Disney’s Pinocchio, as well as appearing in the related music video. The song charted at number fifty-two on Billboard’s Hot Dance Club Songs chart, becoming her first and only chart single. It has since peaked at number thirty-seven.

    She also recorded a version of the Olivia Newton-John song “Magic” for the Wizards of Waverly Place soundtrack. Martin has partnered with Build-A-Bear Workshop for their “Love.Hugs.Peace” movement. For the ad campaign, she recorded a special version of “Let’s Talk About Love”. She also recorded two songs in Camp Rock 2: The Final Jam, “Tear it Down” and “Walkin in My Shoes”, together with the Camp Rock co-star Matthew “Mdot” Finley. Martin also appear on the collaborative song “It’s On”, which features the co-stars of the film. All songs appear in the soundtrack of the film.

    Martin appeared in the Demi Lovato’s music video “Remember December”. She did not join the Jonas Brothers Live in Concert tour, with Demi Lovato and the cast of Camp Rock 2: The Final Jam, because she was filming Mean Girls 2.
