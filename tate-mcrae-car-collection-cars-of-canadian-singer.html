    Overview

    Tate Rosner McRae was born on 1 July 2003 in Calgary, Alberta, Canada. She is a singer and dancer by profession. When McRae launched her original song “One Day” in 2017, it went viral on YouTube and got the attention of several music companies. RCA Records approached her in 2019, and subsequently released her debut EP, “All the Things I Never Said” (2020). Tate gained world recognition from her song “You Broke Me First” which became an international hit. Famous songs sang by McRae includes One Day, Hard to Find, Drown, You Broke Me First, and She’s All I Wanna Be.

    Achieving great heights at a very young age, Tate McRae is not behind anyone in buying stylish cars.

    1. 1968 Ford Mustang GT390

    Featured in the CBS drama Blue Bloods this legendary car is a pure masterpiece. Gearheads go wild when they see this 1968 Ford Mustang GT390, driven by Steve McQueen in the 1968 movie. This car is the evidence of classic vintage cars present in McRae’s collection. With a top speed of 105 mph and 0-60 time of 5.9 sec. this car has a stunning 6.4L V8 that produces 325 hp. Its launch price was nearly $3,500 but this masterpiece sold for a whooping price of $3.7 million in 2020.

    2. BMW i8

    About a decade ago, when hybrid supercars were not so famous, BMW introduced i8. One of the most stylish-looking hybrid supercars BMW i8 equips with a 1.5L twin-power turbo inline-3 cylinder engine combined with an eDrive Electric Motor which can power it to 357 hp. Canadian Singer bought this hybrid in 2018 at a staggering price of $148,495 and prefers to drive to her shows. It goes past 155 mph and has an 0-60 mph acceleration time of 4.2 sec.

    3. Chevrolet Suburban

    One of the SUV models manufactured by America-based Chevrolet is the Suburban. It is mainly introduced in the market to give competition to the likes of Cadillac Escalade. Chevrolet Suburban is a budget-friendly full-sized SUV and is one of its favorites of Tate. One of the most reliable SUVs Chevrolet Suburban has a price tag of $60,000 and is also considered as most comfortable. It comes with various engine options like a 5.3L V8 engine and a 6.2L V8 engine which gives this monster a 355 hp and top speed of 154 mph along with a 0-60 mph time of 7.4 seconds.

    4. Audi A8

    The first and only sedan in McRae’s garage is Audi A8. A luxury sedan with top-notch performance and bold design, Tate has A8‘s top model. Available in the market since 1994, the latest model of this sedan equips a 3L V6 engine that generates 335 hp. Peyton added this car in her garage at a price tag of $86,500. Audi A8 attains a top speed of 155 mph and can go quickly to 60 mph in 5.7 sec.
